About us

We are the platform to use whenever and wherever you'd need Halachic Times (Zemanim) according to Hakham Ovadia Yosef zt'l, based on what he held and practiced. This is the digital equivalent to the Ohr Hachaim calendar, which is what Maran zt"l himself made with HaRav Shlomo Benizri and HaRav Asher Darshan.

For areas outside of the USA, we provide an (optional) derivitive of the Ohr Hachaim calendar called the Amudeh Hora'ah calendar, authored by HaRav Leeor Dahan. These shitot differ in times outside of the day's range (such as Dawn & Nightfall) to be complient with R' David Yosef's opinions, accommodating latitudinal deviations from Eretz Yisrael while still using seasonal minutes.

These times are accessible; not just through our website, but also our apps, print-out calendars, downloadable iCal calendars (for digital calendar apps) and TV walls for shuls. For other formats that developers would like to be creative with, all of our math/algorithms are fully open source, with implementations in JavaScript, Java & Swift. Furthermore, anyone looking to calculate it themselves (or just learn how we did it) could learn through the sugya using our descriptions as indexes for the various sources we used.
