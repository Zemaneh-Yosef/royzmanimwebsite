The Holistic Sephirat Ha'Omer

Source: Yalkut Yosef vol. 22 page 85 onwards - 5779 edition | Hazon Ovadia Yom Tov, page 232 onwards

The מצוה is performed best at the beginning of the night (3/4th's of a mil after sunset, but a minyan can do by sunset if needed), thus allowing the weeks to be "complete". This completeness is factored into whether one can say the blessing: if one of the previous days had been forgotten (from its night until the next day's night) or the counting for that day is happening after Dawn, one would not say the blessing.
